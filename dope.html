DOPE

Collaborators: Awa Bousso, Max Eckert, Brian Young, Alliah George, Tiffany Preston

Technology: Final Cut Pro, Professional Audio and Film Equipment
DOPE is an investigative documentary into the drug culture of Accra, Ghana. Though highly illegal, drugs, specifically marijuana, are still known to be easily accessible. DOPE unearths how prevalent drug use actually is, what the effects of drug use are, and what is like to work in this illegal trade.
