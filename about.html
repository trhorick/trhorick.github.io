ABOUT // TYLER RHORICK
Originally from Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, Tyler Rhorick is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Interactive Media Arts (IMA) from NYU Shanghai with a minor in Global China Studies. Studying in Shanghai, Prague, and Accra, Tyler has followed the NYU Shanghai motto of "making the world his major" and has been dedicated to making projects that communicate human stories. Having had a transformative experience from his education and wanting to create a stage for other students to have the same transformation, Tyler will join his alma mater as the Associate of New Student Programs in the Summer of 2017. Tyler is excited to see how his education in tech might be used to better New Student Programs and would love to continue helping any and all students struggling with their IMA projects in the building. Tyler is currently based in Shanghai, China.
