入柜 (Into the Closet)

Special Thanks: Roopa Vasudevan, Scott Fitzgerald, Sun Jingyi, Nicole Chan, NYU Shanghai, and all of the storytellers

Technology: Adobe Premiere, Arduino, RFID Reader/Cards, Automatic Embrodiery Machine, Professional Audio and Film Equipment

Full Documentation
An interactive installation, 入柜, which roughly translates to "Enter the Closet" in English uses documentary film to communicate the stories of Shanghai, China's vibrant and diverse LGBTQ community. Housed within a closet 入柜 welcomes you into the closet and shares stories from both expats and locals about accepting one's self identity, the potential challenges of being LGBTQ, and what it means to "come out" to others. Users enter the closet and can select one of the shirts. Once the user places the shirt on the hook in the center of the room, a hidden RFID reader will read the RFID card sewn into the pocket of the shirt and start a short documentary clip of one of my interviewees.
